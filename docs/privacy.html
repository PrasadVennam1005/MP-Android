Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Local-First Guarantee: MoneyPilot is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your financial transaction logs, budgets, and investment details are stored locally on your device and are never sold or shared with third parties.

1. Overview

MoneyPilot ("we", "our", or "us") provides a personal finance management app designed to help you track expenses, budget your resources, and manage investment portfolios. This Privacy Policy details how we handle, protect, and process user data when using the MoneyPilot application.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect and store information in the following ways to provide and improve the services offered by MoneyPilot:

A. Local Financial Data

All financial logs, budget settings, and account details you input are saved in a secure, local Room database directly on your device. We do not transmit or store this financial data on our servers.

B. Google Account Credentials

If you choose to use the "Continue with Google" feature, we access basic profile details provided by Google Identity Services, including:

  • Your Name (to customize dashboard greetings)
  • Email Address (to verify authentication status and manage optional user accounts)
  • Profile Picture URL (to display your avatar inside the navigation bar)

C. Timezone and Preferences

We check your local device timezone and onboarding preferences (such as preferred currency) to schedule timezone-aligned daily financial news notifications locally. This check happens entirely on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates services that may collect information used to identify you. Below are the third-party providers we utilize:

  • Google Play Services: Used to run background workers and core OS-level interfaces. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Authentication & Analytics: Used to securely manage login credentials, backup sync sessions, and collect anonymous app usage data to optimize responsiveness and diagnose crashes. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention and Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information:

  • Local SQLite databases are restricted to the sandbox storage allocated strictly to MoneyPilot.
  • All transmissions with third-party components (such as authenticating with Firebase or fetching Wealth RSS feeds from CNBC/Economic Times) use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption.
  • Guest pilot data remains completely local. If you sign in with Google, credentials and profile mappings are securely synced via standard Google Play Credential protocols.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

You retain full control over your data. You may delete all transaction records, custom categories, and budgets at any time directly through the app Settings menu ("Clear All Data").

If you wish to revoke Google Sign-In permissions or delete account details from our backup registry, you can disconnect your account inside settings or contact us directly at our support email listed below.
